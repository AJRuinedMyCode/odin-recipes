Jerk Wings

Description

These wings are full of spice and extremely addictive. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 lbs chicken wings
  • 4-5 tbsp BADIA jerk seasoning
  • 2 tbsp complete seasoning
  • 1 tbsp granulated garlic
  • 1-2 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 1 tps ground ginger
  • 1 tps ground allspice
  • 2 tbsp browning
  • 1/3 cup walkerswood(mild or hot) jerk seasoning

Steps

  1. Clean chicken and pat dry
  2. Combine dry ingredients and add to chicken
  3. Add wet ingredients
  4. Massage all seasonings until fully coated
  5. Marinate for at least 1-2 hrs(24 hrs for best results)
  6. Heat oven to 400 degrees
  7. Bake in a greased line baking sheet uncovered for 1-1.5 hrs or until desired crispness

Or air fry 100 degrees for 20-25 mins
